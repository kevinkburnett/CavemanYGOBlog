My Goals & Vision

Attending Michigan State University has been a transformative experience, providing opportunities for both academic and personal growth. These experiences have allowed me to define my goals with clarity and purpose. I am excited to share more about who I am, the professional endeavors I pursue, and the vision I hold for my future.

  • Short-Term Goals

    During my time at the Broad College of Business, I am committed to excelling academically and expanding my understanding of the business world. 

    With a passion for entrepreneurship, my immediate focus is on developing TrailEagle Packs LLC, combining my expertise in finance, business strategy, and outdoor innovation.

     
  • Long-Term Goals

    I am deeply committed to self-improvement and leveraging my skills to create meaningful change. In ten years, I envision myself as a successful CEO.

    My aim is to use my success to empower others, support entrepreneurial growth, and make a positive impact on communities around the world.

     
  • Future Visio   

    My goal is to create a thriving venture that reflects my values and passion for innovation.

    With a lifelong dedication to learning and a mindset ready to tackle challenges, I am confident in building a future filled with opportunity and purpose.
